❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about ClaudConverter Pro.

Is ClaudConverter Pro really free to use?

Yes! We offer a free tier that allows you to perform up to 10 conversions per day with basic features. For unlimited conversions and advanced features, you can upgrade to our Pro or Business plans.

How secure is my data with ClaudConverter Pro?

Your privacy and security are our top priorities. All files are encrypted during transfer and processing. Converted files are automatically deleted from our servers after 24 hours, and we never store or share your data with third parties.

What file formats do you support?

We support over 100 file formats across videos, audio, images, documents, and more. Some popular formats include MP4, MOV, AVI, MP3, WAV, JPG, PNG, PDF, DOCX, XLSX, PPTX, and EPUB. For a complete list, check our supported formats page.

Can I convert multiple files at once?

Yes! Our Pro and Business plans include batch processing, allowing you to convert multiple files simultaneously. This feature is perfect for professionals who need to convert large numbers of files regularly.

Do you offer API access?

API access is available with our Business plan. Our comprehensive API documentation makes it easy to integrate file conversion capabilities directly into your applications or workflows.
