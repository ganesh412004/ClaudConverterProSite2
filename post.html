How to Optimize PDFs for Web

By Jane Smith June 15, 2023

PDF files are widely used for sharing documents online, but they can often be unnecessarily large, leading to slow loading times and poor user experience. In this article, we'll explore several techniques to optimize your PDFs for web publishing.

1. Use the Right Compression Settings

Most PDF creation tools offer compression options. When saving your PDF, look for settings like "Optimize for Web" or "Reduce File Size". These options typically apply a balanced approach to compression that maintains quality while reducing file size.
